Who we are

A modern holding company, built for the long term.

JJ Astronauts is a privately held group that owns and operates consumer brands across commerce, lifestyle, and specialty retail. We are not a fund with an exit clock — we buy businesses to keep them, steward them, and help them compound for decades.

Each brand keeps its own identity, team, and voice. What they share is access to our central platform: capital, operations, technology, customer support, and a network of operators who have done it before.

The result is the independence founders want with the resources of a much larger company behind them.

What we do

Three things we do well.

A simple, repeatable playbook for turning promising brands into enduring ones.

Acquire

We partner with founders and owners of profitable consumer brands, offering a fair deal and a permanent home — not a flip.

Operate

Shared services — finance, supply chain, technology, and 24/7 customer support — free each team to focus on product and growth.

Scale

We reinvest patiently in new channels, new markets, and new products — compounding each brand for the long run.
