Our story

It started with four friends and a lot of curiosity.

We met in high school and never really stopped hanging out. We loved the same things back then that we love now — trying new stuff, building things, exploring, and having a ridiculous amount of fun together.

JJ Astronauts is what happened when we decided to make that our actual job. Here's the part that makes us different: we don't buy brands. We build them from scratch, grow them with care, and then find genuinely amazing people to run them for the long haul.

No exit clocks, no flipping, no fuss. Just a group of friends who like making good things and sticking around to see them thrive.

What we do

We build, we grow, we hand over the keys.

It's pretty simple, honestly. Three things, done with a lot of heart.

Build from scratch

Every brand starts as a blank page and a "wouldn't it be cool if…". We sweat the details, make the first version, and put it out into the world.

Grow it together

We roll up our sleeves — product, supply chain, tech, and always-on customer support — and help each brand find its people.

Find amazing people

Then we hand the keys to operators we trust and back them all the way. When they outgrow us, we couldn't be prouder.

How we roll

We treat this like a family.

Our whole goal is to help the people around us succeed — full stop.

Family, not a portfolio

We look out for each other and for everyone who joins the journey. Wins are shared, and nobody's left to figure it out alone.

We think like teachers

The best feeling? When someone we've worked with overtakes us in skill. We cheer the loudest and show them off like proud parents.

In it for the long run

We're not here for a quick flip. We build things we're proud of and stick around to help them grow for years, not months.
